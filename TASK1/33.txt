In Vanir Space, The Microcosmus, Still Hashing Things Out
"I wasn’t there the night Lady Xiaoli Song was injured in an
explosion," Nathan quietly repeated. "I’m told Ronnie spent nearly half an
hour in rebuilding her brain – growing new brain cells, if what I’d been told
is really correct. He did it out there in the gravel driveway; out in the cold. Lili
said he’d done it perfectly … except the blast had left pieces of her brain
scattered in the gravel. When she suddenly woke up, she only had the memories of
her life at five years of age."
