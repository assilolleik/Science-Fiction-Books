The screams of the dying man alerted the indigenous guards, so it
made very little sense to remain silent any longer. The creature was
blown apart when several rounds hit it, scattering its remains into the
brush. The rest of the squad quickly rushed forward and began
shooting as they went. They were carefully eliminating natives –
starting with those closest to the shed – while few surviving
indigenes escaped further away into the brush.
‘’ Tank thought to himself before he started issuing orders.
"One – Guard! Two, sensors on the door and walls! Four –
Rear guard! The rest, you with me"
