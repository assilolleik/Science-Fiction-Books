‘Given that knowledge,’ he said, ‘mark what we should be able to do! We
should not only be able to use this uranium and thorium; not only should
we have a source of power potent that a man might carry in his hand
the energy to light a city for a year, fight fleet of battleships, or
drive one of our giant liners across the Atlantic; but we should also
have a clue to enable us at last to quicken the process of
disintegration in all the elements, where decay is still so slow
as to escape our finest measurements.
