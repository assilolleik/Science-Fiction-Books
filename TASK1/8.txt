One of the tables contained the motionless professor, strapped down
with a mask over his mouth. Most of the professor’s head had
been shaved clean, making him look even frailer. The mask connected
by a long tube to one of the electronic machines, which in turn was
connected to a tall cylinder. The professor’s chest rose and fell with each
click of the machine.
Miguel noticed the same arrangement was waiting for him next to the
empty operating table. He stood next to the professor, hands folded in prayer like he had seen
his mother do over his dying grandfather
