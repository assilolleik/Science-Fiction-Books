At his elbow, Percival the monkey was asleep in his cage, he arrives having
"just died" as expected. He had copies of all the
signed papers to prove the professor ha donated his body to
science, and more specifically to him. But since he had taken great pains
to restore the professor’s appearance after the operation—complete with
secured toupee—not only was he unlikely to be questioned, there was no
reason not to have a glorious wake attended by hundreds of scientists and
admirers from around the world, all eager to outdo each other with
eulogies of high praise.
